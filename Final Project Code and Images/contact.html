Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create an account on your platform?

A: To create an account, fill out the registration form on our website and submit the required information. You will also need to make a one-time payment during the account registration process.

Q: How can I contact a seller through a phone number?

A: If a seller has provided a phone number in their listing, you can contact them directly through the provided number for more details or to arrange a meetup.

Q: How do I pay and arrange payments?

A: Currently, you can make payments using Cash App, Venmo, Zelle, or in cash. In the future, we plan to implement PayPal as an additional payment option. Ensure to communicate with the seller and choose a secure location, such as on campus.

Q: Is this platform similar to Facebook Marketplace?

A: Yes, our platform operates similarly to Facebook Marketplace. It allows users to buy and sell items within the university community, promoting a safe and convenient marketplace for students.

Q: Are transactions on this platform secure?

A: Yes, we prioritize the security of transactions. Implementing secure payment methods and user verification processes helps ensure a safe buying and selling experience for our users.
