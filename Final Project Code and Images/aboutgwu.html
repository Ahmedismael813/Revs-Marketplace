About Us

Welcome to our REVS Marketplace!

We are a new initiative at GWU, created to help students buy and sell used or new items conveniently without leaving campus. Our mission is to provide a platform for GWU students to connect and trade a wide range of products, from textbooks to electronics, clothing, furniture, and more.

With the REVS Marketplace, you can save time and money by finding great deals within the GWU community. Whether you're looking to declutter your space or find affordable items, our platform is here to assist you.
